Making Biryani

Biryani is mixed rice dish with origins among the muslims
of the indian subcontinets. It's typically cooked with chicken or goat meat. This dish is especially
popular throughout the indian subcontinent, as well as among the disapora from the region. This
is one of the most amazing royal delicasies introduced to us by the mughals.Since then it has been much popular and is considered to be a
luxurious trat to enjoy on a special occasions. Making a chicken biryani in authentic dum style does take some time and needs little expertise. Few things to do before cooking biryani!!!

1. Marination : Marinating chicken with yogurt & spices is the first key step to make the best chicken biryani. DO NOT SKIP Marination as it makes chicken soft. Yogurt Tenderizes the chicken & the spices enhance the Flavour.



2. Preparing Rice : Basmati rice has to be rinced very well a few times, in ample amount of water to get rid of the starch. This prevents the grains of rice from turning mushy.



3. Water : This recipe uses plain water to cook the chicken biryani However it can be replaced with chicken stock or coconut milk.

Preparation for Chicken Biryani!!!

  1. To a bowl,add Curd,Ginger Garlic, paste, 1/2 tbs Salt,Biryani masala powder, Turmeric & Chilli powder.
  2. Mix up well and taste the marinade. Add more salt and spices if needed.
  3. Make gashes on the Chicken add it to the marinade & set aside for 1 hour.
  4. Rince Basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes. Drain to a colander after 30 minutes.

How to make Chicken Biryani::

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a cooker or pot.


  2. Saute whatever whole spices you have (bay-leaf, Cardamom,star anise) etc For a minute.


  3. Fry onions evenly stirring often until light brown


  4. Add marinated Chicken & Saute for 5 minute on a medium Heat.


  5. Cover and cook on a low flame, Until the Chicken is soft cooked or tender.

  6. Saute to evaporate any excess moisture in the pot.


  7. Add Yogurt,Mint,Green Chilli, Red Chilli powder and Garam masala
